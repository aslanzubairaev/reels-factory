Brand style rules:

- niche: web development / programming / discipline / productivity
- audience perception: premium, serious, masculine, modern
- colors: white + orange only (for text, applied by code)
- background: dark, elegant, subtle tech atmosphere
- background reference: dark workspace with monitor, code on screen, warm orange particles at bottom
- face: preserve exactly, no cartoon, no replacement, no stylization
- typography: bold condensed sans-serif (applied by code, not by image model)
- typography reference: Bebas Neue / Anton / Montserrat ExtraBold
- composition: subject center or center-right, text area in lower third
- lower third: must be dark enough for white/orange text readability
- lighting: subtle rim light, monitor glow, warm accent — NOT flat, NOT overexposed

AVOID:
- childish or playful elements
- gaming thumbnail aesthetic
- random neon overload
- fake person or illustrated face
- cartoon / anime / comic style
- stock photo feel
- any text baked into the generated image
- cluttered or busy backgrounds
- overly dark images where person is barely visible
- multiple people or duplicated subjects
